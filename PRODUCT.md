# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Primary users are developers who need a dependable place to review and act on activity across their work.

## Product Purpose

dev-stream brings notifications from agents, GitHub, Linear, Jira, and custom in-house tools into one local-first stream. It helps developers miss fewer updates and resume work without holding every pending item in their head.

## Positioning

One extensible stream that preserves the context, history, filtering, and actions developers need to turn scattered updates into the next step.

## Brand Personality

Opinionated, functional, flexible. The interface should make filtering, scanning history, and handing work to a script, agent, or manual workflow feel direct and dependable.

## Anti-references

Avoid Linear-like product styling, generic “modern AI” trends, dark-mode-by-default visual language, and Threads-like social-feed treatment.

## Design Principles

- Keep attention externalized: the stream must be a trustworthy memory, not another inbox to manage.
- Preserve context with every update so decisions do not require reconstructing history elsewhere.
- Make triage and action faster than switching among source systems.
- Let custom tools participate without forcing them into a proprietary plugin model.
- Use strong defaults while keeping filters, views, and actions adaptable to individual workflows.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG AA and preserve full keyboard operation. No additional requirements are currently specified.
